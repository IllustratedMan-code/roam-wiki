#+title: Differential Equations


Differential Equations can be used to model the change of values based
on equations that model their derivatives, that is, their rate of
change.




simple chemical reactions can be modeled like so:

\[X + Y \mathop{\leftrightarrows}_{k_-}^{k_+} Z \]

$k_-$ represents the rate of dissociation, whereas $k_+$ represents
the rate of reaction.

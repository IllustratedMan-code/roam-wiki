#+title: immunology-terms


* Null Allele
- Non-functional allele

* IgX/g/h/e
- Immunoglobulin X

* Ectopic Expression
- Abnormal expression (ecto = out of, topic = place)

* Cytokine
- Loose definition encompassing chemicals (small proteins) that are
  used to communicate
  with other cells (most typically by immune cells).
- [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cytokine][Wikipedia]]

** Belmont report
- 3 principles
  - Respect for persons: people are autonomous agents generally, but
    sometimes cannot self-determine, in which case, they require
    protection
  - Beneficience: do not harm, maximize possible benefits and minimize
    possible harms
  - Justice: Benefits should not be denied to those who need them, treatments
    should not be tested on persons who will not be in the beneficiary group.
** Research misconduct
- Research misconduct is defined as fabrication, falsification, or
  plagiarism
- steps in response: inquiry, investigation, adjudication

** Records based research
- understand specific risks, minimize risks, obtain approvals
- Risks: privacy, confidentiality
- Certificate of confidentiality (Coc) prevents disclosure of research
  data through legal proceedings

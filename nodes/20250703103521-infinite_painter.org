#+title: Infinite Painter

Infinite painter is essentially [[id:e296ce81-5f8a-4cce-bbaf-e404dd947567][Krita]] but is not free or open source. However,
Infinite painter is particularly well set up for use with a touch screen, making it
more ideal for Android. Since I have an android tablet, I use this app.

#+title: ATAC-seq

ATAC-seq is an[[id:39ab05a9-a025-44f9-b652-a2ce4a5330a6][ in vivo]] [[id:4550b3f0-11b9-486d-94ee-09fbfea877d9][biological assay]] used to determine how accessible chromatin is.
It measures this by utilizing an enzyme called Tn5.

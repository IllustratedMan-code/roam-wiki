#+title: topisomerase
#+filetags: TODO


Cuts DNA, regulating supercoiling of DNA. DNA is cut at supercoil, and
the DNA unwravels until it hits a low enough free energy (at which
point it doesn't rotate). At this point, DNA repair mechanisms are
triggered, connecting the DNA backtogether.

#+title: Gene Regulation


Genes are not all transcribed equally, and the resulting mRNA is not equally stable.

One of the main contributors to gene regulation is a collection of proteins called
[[id:06798ec2-b3a9-4c6b-a1e7-3b65de64a5c1][Transcription Factors]].
